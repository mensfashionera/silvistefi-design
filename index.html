About Silvi

Architect by training.
Artist by soul.

Silvi is an architect and artist in Washington DC who animates her surroundings by creating positive, innovative and inspiring work. She pursued her love of design at the University of Virginia, earning her Bachelor and Master's degrees in Architecture.

Late nights and weekends find her hard at work in her apartment-turned-studio, pouring her love of color, texture, and movement into works of a different scale.

Creative Philosophy

How I work

Drawing inspiration from the commonplace — objects, nature, people, and emotions — every painting tells a deeply personal story through abstraction and texture.

🎨

Color & Texture

Each work is built layer by layer into a rich tactile landscape using a variety of materials, recyclables, natural elements and found objects.

✦

Bold Abstraction

Characterized by the purposeful abstraction of contemporary design married with the richness of traditional fine art.

◈

Custom Commissions

Every commissioned work begins with a conversation. Silvi collaborates closely to create pieces that feel personal, permanent, and profound.
